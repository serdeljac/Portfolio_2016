A little about me...

Keeping it simple, I'm a tech geek who loves custom, dynamic computer systems & interfaces and desires to express ingenuity in web technologies. So why did I decide to go into web design and development? Generally, it’s because I love being creative, enjoy talking and collaborating ideas with others, and scripting is just too much fun (till the bugs come in).

Since late 2014, I have been getting back into web design and development by taking various courses at BCIT, learning the latest web technologies, UI/UX styles, and creating responsive web designs from scratch. Now I feel confident in my work and abilities to take on professional project work as a front-end web designer.

My objectives are pretty basic: to design and develop more client projects and to gain further experience within the web development field. I often study and experiment with different web technologies and designs; posting my techniques and references on my blog. What’s most important is working together with back-end developers and learning more about developing server-side scripting.
